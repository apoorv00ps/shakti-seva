About SHAKTI SEVA

SHAKTI SEVA is a government-integrated portal designed to formally recognize and support informal, unorganized, and day-pay workers. It bridges the gap between workers and official recognition, providing them with opportunities for upskilling and a platform to address grievances.

The portal operates on three core pillars: Registration (formal recognition), Upskilling (skill development), and Grievance Redressal (query resolution). All powered by government-grade security and transparency.
